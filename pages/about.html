Meet the Yeti Family

The Dad

Josh is originally from Utah, but lucky for Loni, he was a nomad and moved around the country for years and happened to be in Florida when she needed him the most. He is an amazing husband and dad. By day he's a warehouse manager for a rad ring company. During his free time he can typically be found hiking, relaxing by the fire pit, or playing games with family and friends.

The Mom

Loni was born and raised in Florida but has acclimated to Utah over the past 7 years. She loves her job working as a project manager for a not-for-profit health insurance company. When she has free time, she is reading, watching football, or hanging out with the family.

The Kid

Gage is the son of Josh and Loni. He was born in Florida but lived there for only the first year of his life. He is in the 3rd grade. His hobbies are art, video games, and building things. He desperately to start a YouTube channel with his dad.
